# Tradeoffs

- RLDS source format may not be regenerated exactly for every dataset.
- LeRobot-style conversion may omit, flatten, or transform unsupported modalities into Parquet vectors.
- Robo-DM-style fallback is not official Robo-DM.
- Video codec availability can affect results.
- Sample-capped results are not full-paper-scale results.
- Hardware strongly affects throughput and RAM.
